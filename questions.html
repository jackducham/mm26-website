FAQ

What is Mechmania?
Mechmania is a 24-hour hackathon that occurs annually as part of the Reflections Projections conference.
Competitors will create AI to compete against one another in a game that will be revealed on the day of the competition.
1st, 2nd, and 3rd place teams will recieve a prize (that is a secret until the competition!)
What are these pages?
The Twitch link will take you to the livestream of currently running games (or commentary).
The FAQ is this page. FAQ means "Frequently Asked Questions."
The Wiki will help you actually participate in the event and is meant to help get you started.
